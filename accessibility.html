Accessibility Statement

Last updated: June 2026

Ms Renovations Pro is committed to making our website usable by as many people as possible, including those who use assistive technologies such as screen readers, keyboard navigation, and screen magnification.

What we've done

We aim to follow the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 at Level AA as a guide. Steps we've taken include:

  • Semantic page structure with landmarks and a "skip to content" link.
  • Keyboard support for all interactive elements, including our before/after comparison slider.
  • Visible focus indicators for keyboard users.
  • Text and background color combinations checked for sufficient contrast.
  • Descriptive alternative text for meaningful images.
  • Respect for the "reduce motion" setting in your operating system or browser.

Accessibility tools on this site

Use the accessibility button at the bottom-right of any page to:

  • Increase text size.
  • Turn on high contrast.
  • Switch to a more readable font.
  • Highlight links.
  • Reduce motion and animations.

Your choices are saved on your device for future visits.

A note on accessibility overlays. We deliberately do not use automatic "accessibility overlay" widgets that claim instant compliance. Instead, we build accessibility into the site itself and review it over time. We believe this serves visitors better.

Ongoing effort

Accessibility is an ongoing process, not a one-time fix. As we add content and features, we work to keep them accessible. We may not yet be perfect, and we welcome your feedback.
