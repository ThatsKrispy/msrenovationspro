Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Ms Renovations Pro ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect through our website, how we use it, and the choices you have. This is a general policy and not legal advice; for specific compliance questions, consult an attorney.

Information we collect

We collect information in two ways:

  • Information you give us. When you call us or submit a request for a quote, you may share your name, phone number, email, address, and details about your project.
  • Information collected automatically. If you accept analytics cookies, we use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our site — for example, which pages are viewed and how people arrive. This data is aggregated and used to improve the site.

Cookies and your choices

When you first visit, we ask whether you accept analytics cookies. Analytics cookies are only set if you click "Accept." If you click "Decline," no analytics cookies are placed. You can clear your saved choice at any time by clearing your browser's site data, which will prompt the banner again.

We do not sell your personal information.

How we use your information

  • To respond to quote requests and provide our services.
  • To contact you about your project.
  • To understand and improve how our website performs (only with your consent).

Sharing

We share information only as needed to run our business — for example, with service providers like Google Analytics (when you consent) — or when required by law. We do not sell or rent your information to third parties for their marketing.

Data retention

We keep project and contact information for as long as needed to serve you and meet our legal and business obligations, then dispose of it securely.

Your rights
